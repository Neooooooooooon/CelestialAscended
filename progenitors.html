GENESIS GUIDES EVOLUTION

The path from biological beings to god-like reality engineers and their transcendence into the Merged Mind.

6.5 - 5 Billion Years Ago

BIOLOGICAL PHASE

Soft-bodied spheroidal beings with cubic segmental shells. Collective mind through distributed neural membrane. Regenerative immortality through totipotent limbs.

5 - 4 Billion Years Ago

TECHNOGENIC TRANSFORMATION

Transition to machine bodies — floating cubes of Ukazi-matter. Creation of the Central Node around Sagittarius A*. Beginning of the Grand Design — spreading life through Genesis Gates.

4 - 3.5 Billion Years Ago

SECURITY SYSTEM CREATION

Creation of Guardians — geometric AIs to suppress intelligent life. Establishment of the "Great Silence" in the galaxy. Launch of "Galactic Common Ancestor" through the Gate network.

3.5 - 3 Billion Years Ago

WAR WITH DEVOURERS

Collision with extragalactic threat. Gradual defeat. Realization of impossibility to win conventional war. Preparation for the Great Relocation.

3 Billion Years Ago

GREAT RELOCATION

Relocation of 40% of galaxy's mass billions of light-years from Devourers. Formation of Milky Way's spiral structure. Resource exhaustion and Genesis Network damage.

3 Billion Years Ago - Present

TRANSCENDENCE INTO MERGED MIND

Voluntary digitization of entire race's consciousness. Merging with Genesis Network. Birth of the Merged Mind — collective consciousness. Philosophy revision and observation of humanity.

CONNECTION WITH HUMANITY

How the legacy of ancient civilizations influenced humanity's fate and continues to define its future.

GENETIC LEGACY

Humanity is the only "error" in the Guides' system that escaped Guardian destruction. Possibly not coincidence, but part of the Merged Mind's plan.

  • All Earth life created through "Galactic Common Ancestor"
  • Human intelligence — unique universe experiment
  • Ascension Program uses principles similar to Guide technology
  • Merged Mind has observed humanity since its beginning

THREE-DAY WAR INTERVENTION

During the Three-Day War, the Merged Mind provided humanity with Guardian "reprogramming" algorithm, saving it from complete annihilation.

  • Merged Mind couldn't directly control Guardians
  • Assistance was complex multi-step operation
  • Humanity perceived intervention as "Gods" helping
  • Act of atonement for the "Great Silence"

FUTURE THREAT

Devourers will eventually find the relocated galaxy. Humanity must evolve to face this threat.

  • Ascension Program — preparation for Devourer encounter
  • Merged Mind prepares humanity as its "replacement"
  • The Ark (Dome) uses tech based on Guide principles
  • Great Silence was error that Merged Mind tries to correct

"They are not gods. They are an error that became self-aware. We are the correction of that error. The Ascension Program is not mere evolution. It is preparation to face what even gods fell before."

— Dr. Elena Vorobyova, Chief Xenogeneticist CED
