ERA ARCHIVES

Comprehensive documentation of each historical period in human and galactic history.

PROGENITOR ERA

6.5 Billion - 4 Billion BCE
The dawn of intelligent life in our galaxy. The Progenitors (Genesis Guides) emerged as the first and only intelligent species before humanity. Over 2.5 billion years, they evolved from biological entities into transcendent machine beings, building the Genesis Gates network and establishing the Great Silence protocol that would shape galactic history forever.
6.5B BCE

Emergence of Progenitor Civilization

The first intelligent life forms appear in what was then an elliptical galaxy. These cube-shelled beings possess collective consciousness and regenerative abilities, laying the foundation for their unique philosophy of cosmic order.

5B BCE

Genesis Gates Construction

The Progenitors begin constructing the network of Genesis Gates - instantaneous portals connecting habitable worlds across the galaxy. This marks their transition from planetary to galactic civilization.

4B BCE

The Great Silence Protocol

To maintain cosmic order and prevent chaotic evolution of life, the Progenitors create the Guardians and implement the Great Silence - a system that suppresses any emerging intelligent life after it reaches a certain threshold.

EXPANSION ERA

4 Billion BCE - 2024 CE
A period of galactic-scale engineering and eventual decline. The Progenitors reshape the elliptical galaxy into the spiral Milky Way we know today, fleeing from the extragalactic threat known as the Devourers. Their civilization ultimately transcends physical form, becoming the Merged Mind that would later observe humanity's development.
4B BCE

Galactic Reconstruction

Using unimaginable technologies, the Progenitors transform their elliptical galaxy into a spiral structure - the nascent Milky Way. This act of cosmic engineering represents the peak of their civilization's power.

3.8B BCE

War with the Devourers

The Progenitors encounter the extragalactic Devourers - entities that consume galaxies to combat universal entropy. Despite their advanced technology, the Progenitors face inevitable defeat.

3.7B BCE

Transcendence to Merged Mind

Facing extinction, the Progenitors perform a collective act of digitization, merging all individual consciousnesses into the Merged Mind - a single entity that inhabits the Genesis Gates network, observing the galaxy in silence.

SILENCE ERA

2024 - 2037 CE
Humanity's first encounter with Progenitor technology. The discovery of the Alps Anomaly marks the beginning of a new chapter in human history, as Celestial Exp Inc (CEI) uncovers ancient secrets buried for billions of years, setting humanity on a collision course with the galaxy's hidden guardians.
2024 CE

Alps Anomaly Discovery

During drilling operations in the Alps, workers discover a perfectly smooth structure of unknown material buried 12 kilometers underground. Celestial Exp Inc identifies it as a Genesis Gate - a 4-billion-year-old artifact of the Progenitors.

2025-2036 CE

Genesis Gate Research

CEI conducts extensive research on the Genesis Gate, attempting to understand its purpose and operation. The scientific community is divided between excitement and caution regarding the ancient technology.

2037 CE

Gate Activation Breakthrough

After 13 years of research, CEI scientists successfully activate the Genesis Gate, revealing its function as an instantaneous portal to other worlds. Humanity stands on the brink of a new era of exploration.

DISCOVERY ERA

2037 - 2224 CE
Humanity's golden age of expansion through the Genesis Gates. Celestial Exp Inc transforms into the Human Alliance, a planetary government that leads humanity to technological heights through reverse-engineering of Progenitor artifacts. Population reaches 17 billion, but the "Great Silence" - the absence of other intelligent life - becomes increasingly ominous.
2037 CE

First Gate Transit

The first human team travels through the activated Genesis Gate, arriving on an Earth-like planet in another star system. This marks humanity's first step into interstellar travel without conventional spacecraft.

2045 CE

Formation of Human Alliance

CEI dissolves as a corporate entity, transferring its assets and authority to the newly formed Human Alliance - a planetary government headquartered in Washington D.C., unifying Earth under a single administrative body.

2100-2224 CE

Age of Prosperity

Humanity enjoys nearly two centuries of unprecedented growth and technological advancement. Through reverse-engineering of Progenitor artifacts found on other worlds, human technology advances by leaps, though true understanding of the ancient science remains elusive.

ISOLATION ERA

2224 - 2347 CE
The Three-Day War shatters humanity's golden age. The Guardians - ancient Progenitor defense systems - awaken and nearly exterminate humanity before intervention by the Merged Mind. With 3 billion dead and the Gates sealed, humanity enters an era of forced isolation, realizing that survival requires fundamental evolution rather than expansion.
2224 CE

The Three-Day War

Massive activity around the Genesis Gates triggers the ancient Guardian defense systems. For 72 hours, humanity faces systematic extermination by indestructible geometric AI entities before the Merged Mind intervenes with a reprogramming algorithm.

2225 CE

Gates Sealed, Isolation Begins

In the war's aftermath, all Genesis Gates are emergency-sealed. The Human Alliance establishes the Earth Defense Division to monitor the dormant Gates and protect against future Guardian activity.

2230 CE

Celestial Engineering Directorate Founded

The Human Alliance creates CED, combining intelligence, scientific research, and space exploration functions. Their mission: develop the Ascension Program to evolve humanity into a form capable of surviving in a hostile galaxy.

ASCENSION ERA

2347 CE - PRESENT
The current age of human evolution. Under CED guidance, humanity focuses inward, developing the Ark Project - a massive artificial ecosystem where alien DNA is studied and the Ascension Program tests biological, cybernetic, and hybrid paths to human enhancement. The goal: create a new form of humanity capable of reopening the Gates and facing what lies beyond.
2347 CE

Ark Project Initiation

CED activates the "Habitat Protocol" Directive of the Genesis Gates, causing the Gates themselves to construct the Dome - a massive enclosed ecosystem that becomes the Ark, humanity's laboratory for evolution.

2350 CE

First Successful Ascension

The first human volunteer successfully integrates alien DNA through the biological Ascension path, demonstrating enhanced regeneration and environmental adaptation capabilities without loss of consciousness.

PRESENT DAY

Ascension Program Expansion

The Ark Project operates at full capacity with eight distinct biomes, each simulating different alien environments. Over 500 volunteers have undergone Ascension procedures across biological, cybernetic, and hybrid paths under CED supervision.
